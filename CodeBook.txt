
The list of variables in the course project tidy data file.

Fields 3 through 68 are from signals from the accelerometer and gyroscope
from a smartphone worn by a subject performing the activity in field 2. Variables
starting with t are from the time domain and those starting with f are from
the frequency domain. (See the README.txt file that came with the data for
more information).

See http://www.starlino.com/imu_guide.html for information on how an
accelerometer and gyroscope work in smart phones.
